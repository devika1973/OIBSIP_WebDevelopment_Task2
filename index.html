Biography

Ratan Naval Tata was born on December 28, 1937, in Mumbai, India. He studied architecture at Cornell University and later joined the Tata Group. His early experience at Tata Steel, working with factory workers, shaped his humble leadership style.

In 1991, he became the Chairman of Tata Sons and transformed the Tata Group into a global powerhouse. Under his leadership, Tata Motors, Tata Steel, TCS and Tata Global Beverages expanded internationally.

He is known for the Tata Nano, and major acquisitions such as Jaguar Land Rover and Corus Steel. Even after retirement, he continues inspiring startups and supporting social causes.
